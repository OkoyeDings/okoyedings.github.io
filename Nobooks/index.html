Introducing No’Books

The ground-breaking innovation in education in West Africa. No’Books is a tablet application for taking handwritten notes and assessments.

Features

Digital notetaking

Students write with their hands, maintaining psychomotor learning.

Multimedia integration

Teachers include images, audio and videos in their lesson notes/plans.

Personalized feedback

Teachers provide streamlined individual feedback.

Collaborative learning

Students learn and work together irrespective of distance.

Data driven decisions

Teachers provide data driven individual feedback for each student.

Faster curriculum completion

Deliver curriculum contents in time without sacrificing teaching efficiency.
